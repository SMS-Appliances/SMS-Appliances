Helpful Answers Before You Book

Practical details that can help you prepare for a repair visit and know what to expect.

How fast can a technician usually arrive?

Availability depends on your location and schedule for the day, but SMA Appliances prioritizes urgent appliance problems and may offer same-day appointments when possible.

Can you tell me if the repair is worth it before doing the work?

Yes. The technician will inspect the appliance, explain the issue, and help you understand whether repair or replacement makes more sense.

Do I need to know the appliance model number before calling?

It helps, but it is not required. If you have the brand and model number available, it can help the technician prepare faster.

What should I do before the technician arrives?

Clear space around the appliance, remove items if possible, and make sure the appliance is accessible. For refrigerators or freezers, keep food stored safely if cooling has stopped.

Can you work on built-in or high-end appliances?

Yes, SMA Appliances can service many built-in and premium appliance types. Call with the brand and issue so the technician can confirm the best next step.

What if my appliance starts working again before the appointment?

You can still have it checked, especially if the issue has happened more than once. Intermittent problems often come back and can become worse over time.

Do you carry parts on the truck?

Technicians may carry common replacement parts, but some repairs require ordering the exact part for your appliance model.

Will the technician explain the problem to me?

Yes. SMA Appliances focuses on honest diagnoses and clear explanations before moving forward with repair work.

Can I text photos or a video of the issue?

Yes. Sending a photo, short video, or error code can help the technician understand the problem before arriving.

What information should I include when requesting service?

Include your name, phone number, city, appliance type, brand if known, and a short description of what the appliance is doing.
